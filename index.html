Good to
Know

SXFestival is a two-day festival at the intersection of pleasure, culture, and technology. It's part conference — with real speakers giving real talks — part expo with 50+ brands, and part party. It is not a sex club. It is not a rave. It is a space where adults who are curious about intimacy, technology, and creative expression can explore in a safe, consent-driven environment.
Yes. Many of our visitors are curious newcomers. You don't need to be part of any specific scene or community. Nobody will pressure you to do anything, see anything, or be anything you don't want to be. Come as curious as you like and take it at your own pace.
For the daytime festival: no dress code. Come however you feel comfortable. For the Red Moon Party on Saturday evening, we encourage you to dress to express — think dark, bold, or whatever makes you feel most like yourself. Full details on the ticket confirmation email.
Consent is non-negotiable at SXFestival. We have a clear code of conduct: ask before touching, respect every no, and look after each other. A trained awareness team is present throughout the venue and event. Anyone who does not respect these principles will be removed without refund.
Yes. SXFestival is strictly 18+. Please bring valid ID. Anyone who cannot prove they are 18 or over will not be admitted and will not receive a refund.
